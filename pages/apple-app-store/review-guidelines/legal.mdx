# Legal Guidelines

Following legal guidelines is **not optional**. Ensure your app complies with all local laws and respects users' rights.

### Privacy Policy
- Include a privacy policy that details what user data you collect and how it's used.

### Data Collection and Storage
- Comply with GDPR, CCPA, and other regional data protection laws.

### Intellectual Property
- Ensure all content within your app is either owned by you or properly licensed.

For all the legal requirements your app needs to meet: [App Store Legal
Considerations](https://developer.apple.com/app-store/review/guidelines/#legal)
