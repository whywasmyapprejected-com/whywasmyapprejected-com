# Design Guidelines

Good design is not just about aesthetics; it's about creating an intuitive and
seamless user experience.

### User Interface
- The app's UI should be intuitive, consistent, and visually appealing.

### Usability
- Make sure your app is simple to navigate and does not confuse users.

### Accessibility
- Support various accessibility features to ensure inclusiveness for people with
  disabilities.

Dive deeper into the design prerequisites: [Design Guidelines
Overview](https://developer.apple.com/app-store/review/guidelines/#design)
